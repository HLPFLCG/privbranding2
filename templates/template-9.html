Philosophy

The nature of the sound

PRIV's sound is a journey into the dark, nightmarish realm of Oblivion—a place where medieval echoes meet nature's untamed, ominous beauty. Drawing influence from the daedric landscapes of ancient lore, channeling an otherworldly tradition of storytelling where vocals and melodies intertwine.

Oblivion is where nightmarish creatures dwell, and where hopes and dreams descend to perish. That ancient energy lives in every composition, echoing through the halls of forgotten realms.
