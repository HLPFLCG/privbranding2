The Descent

PRIV's sound is a journey into the dark, nightmarish realm of Oblivion—a place where medieval echoes meet nature's untamed, ominous beauty. The music draws from the daedric landscapes of ancient lore, channeling an otherworldly tradition where vocals and melodies intertwine with vulnerable, introspective intention.

Oblivion is where nightmarish creatures dwell... and where people's hopes and dreams go to die. That energy permeates every track—the weight of forgotten realms, melodic darkness, and the feeling of being lost in a desolate, abandoned world.
