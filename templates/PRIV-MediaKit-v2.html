Brand Philosophy

PRIV's sound is a journey into the dark, nightmarish realm of Oblivion—a place where medieval echoes meet nature's untamed, ominous beauty. Drawing influence from the daedric landscapes of ancient lore, PRIV channels an otherworldly tradition of storytelling in how vocals and melodies intertwine—feeling this introspective longing with often vulnerable intentions.

Oblivion is a place where nightmarish creatures live... and where people's hopes and dreams go to die. That's the kind of energy brought to every track—the feeling of the dark and near, of melodic landscapes and potent musical immensities, with the nature of interconnected feeling of hopes and dreams lost in a desolate, forgotten world.
